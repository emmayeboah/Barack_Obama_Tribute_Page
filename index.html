Here's a time line of Barack Obama

  • 1961 -Born and raised in Hawaii
  • 1964 -His parents divorced
  • 1979 -Obama graduated from Punahou School, an elite college preparatory academy in Honolulu.
  • 1983 -He received a Bachelor Degree in Political Science
  • 1985 -Obama became Community Organizer on Chicago's longely impoverished Far South Side
  • 1991 -Graduated from Harvard University's law school, where He was the first African American to serve as President of Harvard Law Review
  • 1992 -Obama married Michelle Robinson
  • 2005-2008 -Obama represented Illinois in the U.S Senate
  • 2009 - He was awarded the Nobel Prize for his extraordinary efforts to strenthen international diplomacy and cooperation between peoples.
  • 2009-2017 -President of the United State of America
